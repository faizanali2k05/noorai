NoorAI is Pakistan's first agentic marketplace connecting families with verified special-needs therapists — using a pipeline of seven AI agents to handle intent, discovery, ranking, pricing, booking, and follow-up.

Why NoorAI

An emotional, high-trust domain where personalization actually matters.

🧠

Built for specialists

Speech, occupational, behavioral, and physical therapists — matched to a child's specific profile, not a generic search bar.

🤝

Family-first ranking

Eight weighted factors including specialization, distance, language, availability, and parent reviews. Transparent reasoning, no black boxes.

⚡

Agentic by default

Powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash. Agents talk to each other so families just describe their need — the system does the rest.

The seven-agent pipeline

From "I need help" to a booked session with a follow-up loop.

  1. 1
    Intent

    Parses the parent's request into structured therapy needs.

  2. 2
    Discovery

    Finds candidate therapists across the marketplace.

  3. 3
    Ranking

    Scores candidates against 8 factors with explainable weights.

  4. 4
    Pricing

    Resolves session pricing, packages, and subsidy eligibility.

  5. 5
    Booking

    Locks in availability and confirms the slot end-to-end.

  6. 6
    Notification

    Keeps both family and therapist in sync via the app.

  7. 7
    Follow-up

    Handles disputes, reschedules, and feedback after the session.
